ABOUT THE SHOP

D' Extraordinary was founded in Bonifacio Global City by Mr. Daren Ross Gido. A business owner who believes that clothing can bring a confidence and can make you feel extraordinary. Thus the brand really wants to showcase the fashion for all of kinds of people and for all of ages. Where it can bring the confidence that hides in yourself.

Our goal is to lead the world in fashion outerwear and knits by fostering creativity and innovation to ensure complete client pleasure. We aspire to be a responsible corporate citizen in our community as well as a kind and well-run company for our clients, consumers, and staff.
